About Fit4MedRob

Fit4MedRob is a national initiative funded by the PNC aiming to enhance research and innovation in medical robotics. The initiative brings together multiple universities and research centers to develop intelligent robotic systems that support healthcare professionals and improve patient outcomes in rehabilitation, surgery, and diagnostics.
