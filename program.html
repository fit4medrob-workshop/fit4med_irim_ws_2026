Detailed Program

Introduction by the Organizers

Opening remarks and introduction to the workshop rationale, with a focus on the role of Fit4MedRob in promoting robotics and artificial intelligence for rehabilitation and on the contribution of young researchers involved in the project.

The organizers will introduce the workshop structure, the main objectives of the session, and the motivation behind dedicating a specific space to PhD students and early-career researchers who have contributed to Fit4MedRob activities. The introduction will frame the discussion around current achievements, lessons learned, and future directions beyond the project.
